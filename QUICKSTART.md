# PricePulse Quick Start Guide

Get started with PricePulse in 5 minutes!

## Installation (2 minutes)

### Chrome/Edge/Brave

1. Open your browser's extensions page:
   - Chrome: `chrome://extensions/`
   - Edge: `edge://extensions/`
   - Brave: `brave://extensions/`

2. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in top-right)

3. Click "Load unpacked"

4. Select the `pricepulse` folder

5. Done! The extension is now installed.

## Generate Icons (Optional - 1 minute)

1. Open `icons/generate-icons.html` in your browser
2. Download all three icons (16x16, 48x48, 128x128)
3. Save them in the `icons` folder
4. Reload the extension

**Note:** Extension works without this step, but icons make it prettier!

## First Use (2 minutes)

### Track Your First Item

1. Visit any shopping website (Amazon, eBay, etc.)
2. Go to a product page
3. Click the PricePulse icon in your toolbar
4. Click "Track This Price"
5. Done! You're now tracking that item.

### Check Your Dashboard

1. Click the PricePulse icon anytime
2. See all your tracked items
3. View price changes and statistics
4. Click "Visit" to go to any product

### Configure Settings (Optional)

1. Click the settings icon (gear) in the popup
2. Adjust preferences:
   - Check interval (default: 1 hour)
   - Notification settings
   - Price change threshold
3. Click "Save Settings"

## Tips

1. **Pin the Extension**: Click the puzzle icon, then pin PricePulse for easy access

2. **Set Realistic Intervals**: Checking every hour is usually enough

3. **Use Filters**: Filter tracked items by price changes (drops, increases, stable)

4. **Export Data**: Backup your data regularly via Settings

5. **Check History**: Review price trends before making purchases

## Troubleshooting

### Price Not Detected?
- Refresh the page
- Wait a few seconds for dynamic content
- Try a different product page

### Not Getting Notifications?
- Check browser notification permissions
- Verify notifications are enabled in settings
- Ensure price change exceeds your threshold

### Extension Not Working?
- Reload the extension from extensions page
- Check browser console for errors
- Verify all files are present
